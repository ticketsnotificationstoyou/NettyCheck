Q: What protocols are supported in Secure Tunnel?

A: NettyCheck supports Shadowsocks, VLESS, VMESS, and Trojan protocols. These are industry-standard secure proxy protocols commonly used in enterprise environments for testing network configurations and validating secure connection implementations.

Q: Does NettyCheck provide proxy servers or VPN services?

A: No. NettyCheck is a diagnostic client application, not a service provider. You must configure your own server endpoints with appropriate credentials and encryption keys. We do not operate, provide, manage, rent, sell, or have any affiliation with proxy or VPN service providers. All connection endpoints are user-configured for testing purposes only.

Q: Who uses secure proxy protocols?

A: Network engineers, system administrators, and developers use these protocols to test secure proxy implementations, validate connection parameters, diagnose routing configurations, benchmark protocol performance, and verify encryption settings in controlled enterprise and development environments.

Q: Where do I get server credentials?

A: Users must deploy and manage their own proxy server infrastructure or have legitimate access credentials to servers they are authorized to test. NettyCheck only provides the client-side testing capability - you are responsible for all server-side infrastructure and authorization.

Q: Is my configuration data private?

A: Yes. NettyCheck operates entirely locally on your device with zero data collection. Your server addresses, encryption keys, and connection parameters are stored only on your device and never transmitted to us. We have no backend infrastructure and cannot access your configurations. See our Privacy Policy.

Q: How do other diagnostic tools work?

A: All diagnostic tools (Ping Test, DNS Lookup, Speed Test, etc.) operate locally on your device. Simply tap any tool card on the main screen, enter the required parameters (hostname, URL, etc.), and run the test. Results are displayed immediately and stored only on your device.

Q: What are the system requirements?

A: NettyCheck requires iOS 15.0 or later. All features work on iPhone and iPad. Some diagnostic tools may require specific network permissions which the app will request when needed.
